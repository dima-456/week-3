Critique #5

Brief + low-fidelity wireframes

Submit a PDF and/or screenshots of your portfolio project brief and rough wireframes of your site layout. These wireframes can be created using any software, but the emphasis should be on page structure and interaction, and not on design specifics. Consider what you will be showcasing in your portfolio and how you can best represent those mediums.

Critique #4

Artist statement rough drafts

Write an artist statement for your online portfolio. An artist statement is both different and similar to a biography. The main difference is that it needs to articulate your primary focus as an artist. Describe your direction, your mediums, and the meaning and messages to aim to convey in your work. An artist statement needs to be concise; 200-300 words is plenty. You may wish to illustrate how your ideals apply to a specific example of work.

Due March 4
